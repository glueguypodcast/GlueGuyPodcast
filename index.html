The Show

What Is
Glue Guy?

The glue guy is the role player who holds everything together without getting the credit. On the court, in the office, at home. That's the show. That's the audience. Hosted by Cannon Simmons — not a media personality, just a guy with real takes and a genuine love for sports.

Every week: the biggest stories in sports, a guest worth knowing, and takes from real listeners. 45-60 minutes. No fluff.

01
The Rundown
Cannon's solo monologue. The 4–5 biggest things in sports that week plus 1–2 from business, politics, or culture. Opinionated. Confident. Not just informative.
02
The Guest
A guest interesting by virtue of their career or life stage — not necessarily famous, but compelling. Sports is the entry point. Work and life bleed in naturally.
03
Glue Guys
The call-in segment. Listeners send 60–90 second voice memos — sports, culture, parenting, work, life. Cannon plays the audio and reacts live. 3–4 callers per episode.

Your Take

Got a take? Send a 60–90 second voice memo. Sports, culture, parenting, work, whatever. The best ones make the show. Cannon plays the audio and reacts live.

01
Keep it 60–90 seconds. Long enough to have a real take. Short enough to stay sharp.
02
Any topic. Sports is the entry point but not the limit. Work, parenting, pop culture — bring it.
03
Submit within the window. Submissions open 72 hours before recording each week. Timing is posted on social.
04
Be yourself. Don't perform for the mic. Talk like you would in the group chat.
